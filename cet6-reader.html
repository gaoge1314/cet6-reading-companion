🔍 主题预测
五道题围绕"追求个人成功"与"友谊"的关系——文章在批判个人主义文化下牺牲友谊的现象，呼吁重视人际关系。
📌 题型 & 定位词 46-49 细节题 · 50 推断题
定位词：radical individualism · American society's assumption · success stories · preached

🎯 T7 首尾句聚焦
快速扫读每段首尾句，抓文章骨架。只看首尾，不读中间。
📌 第1段 首句 + 尾句

首Many see friendships as a comfort blanket: a shoulder to cry on, a reliable soul to confide in.

尾However, more often than not, in our eagerness to seek individual success, we lose friends along the way and that has become part of the process.

💭 友谊是安慰毯，但追求成功的过程中会失去朋友...
📌 第2段 首句 + 尾句

首The chaos and never-ending turmoil that accompanies everyday life is an aggressive obstacle in the pursuit of stable friendships.

尾As students, we use this excuse to shrug off losing people who we once thought to be loved ones.

💭 日常混乱让友谊很难维持，我们拿这当借口甩掉朋友...
📌 第3段 首句 + 尾句

首Instead of viewing our lives as a race, where everyone is on their own path to some form of success, we must relearn to value the connections we make along the track.

尾We must reevaluate the harm of radical, individual freedom.

💭 不要只把人生当赛跑，要重新审视激进个人主义的危害——作者开始给建议了。
📌 第4段 首句 + 尾句

首Radical individualism elevates the freedom of the individual over the collective.

尾It argues, for instance, that universal healthcare, an example of collectivism, threatened the dream of many Americans.

💭 解释 radical individualism 是啥——个人自由高于集体。
📌 第5段 首句 + 尾句

首Many of us have fallen victim to an individualistic culture.

尾Society tends to celebrate personal achievement and commend it based on the assumption that winning or succeeding is a consequence of working harder than the next person.

💭 我们成了个人主义文化的受害者——社会默认"成功 = 比别人更努力"。
📌 第6段 首句 + 尾句

首As a society, we idolize the concept of "losing friends on the road to success."

尾Invest time in your relationships? Invest time in your work instead.

💭 我们竟然崇拜"为成功失去朋友"！成功学让你把时间花在工作上而不是朋友上。
📌 第7段 首句 + 尾句

首This constant need to succeed has blinded us from seeing through the whirlwind of change that accompanies our everyday lives.

尾In order to succeed, we lose friends, and that has sadly become normalized.

💭 成功让我们瞎了，失去朋友竟然变得"正常"了——挺无奈的。
📌 第8段 首句

首We cannot be complacent with losing friendships because they are our anchors.

💭 不能对失去朋友无所谓——朋友是锚，是定心丸。
📌 第9段 首句 + 尾句

首Yes, pursue success. Challenge yourself. Push limits.

尾doing it alone does not make you more successful or any better than someone who achieved it with the help of others.

💭 作者不是反对成功，而是反对"单打独斗"——跟别人一起成功不丢人。
📌 第10段 首句 + 尾句

首Put effort into fostering healthy relationships, and maintaining them through your busy schedule.

尾having people to support and love you on the way is far more important than any end result.

💭 最后一锤定音：有人爱你支持你，比任何结果都重要。
🎯 中心归纳
文章在批判"个人主义文化下，追求成功=牺牲友谊"的观念。作者认为友谊比成功更重要，朋友是精神支柱，不应该对失去朋友无动于衷。
🎯 用中心做题（T1 中心优先 + T5 排除法）

📌 第4段 → 做47题 T4定位

Radical individualism elevates the freedom of the individual over the collective. It argues, for instance, that universal healthcare, an example of collectivism, threatened the dream of many Americans.

47. What does the author advise people to do about radical individualism?
A) Reexamine its detrimental effects ✅
B) Shrug off the loss of loved ones
C) Avoid the chaos and turmoil involved
D) Remove aggressive obstacles in the way
💭 注意！47题问的是"advise"——作者的建议。第4段只描述了radical individualism是什么，但第3段尾句说"we must reevaluate the harm"——"reevaluate the harm" = A的"Reexamine its detrimental effects"（T3 同义替换）！
📌 第5段 → 做48题 T6段落锁定

Many of us have fallen victim to an individualistic culture. Society tends to celebrate personal achievement and commend it based on the assumption that winning or succeeding is a consequence of working harder than the next person.

48. What is American society's assumption of personal achievement?
A) duly celebrated and highly commended
B) a sure way to fulfill a person's dream
C) revolves around an individualistic culture
D) reflects a person's exceptional endeavor ✅
💭 原文的assumption是"winning = working harder than the next person"——也就是"成功比他人更努力的结果"。"endeavor"就是"努力"！D完美对应assumption的定义。
📌 第6段 → 做49题 T6段落锁定

As a society, we idolize the concept of "losing friends on the road to success." ... Motivational speakers will push their audience to be complacent to the inevitable end of losing friends. Spend too much time with friends? You're losing time better utilized on your achievements. Invest time in your relationships? Invest time in your work instead.

49. What is preached in many success stories?
A) attaching importance to academic gains
B) investing time in work instead of connecting with friends ✅
C) trying to strengthen friendships
D) excelling in academic performance for a prestigious college
💭 原文直接说了"Invest time in your work instead"——把时间花在工作上而不是朋友上。B完美对应！（T3 同义替换）
